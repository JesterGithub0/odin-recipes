No Roux Mac and Cheese

Description:

Mac and cheese doesn't need to be difficult. With this recipe, you'll make a creamy mac and cheese without the need of a roux. Just pasta, milk, butter, and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Milk or cream

Steps:

  1. Boil your macaroni and cheese for 2 minutes less than package instructions
  2. Turn off stove and drain your macaroni
  3. Add macaroni back to pot
  4. Add milk and butter, turn stove to low
  5. Slowly stir in your choice of cheese, add in batches and not all at once to avoid clumps
  6. Enjoy
