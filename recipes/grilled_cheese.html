Simple Grilled Cheese

Description:

A simple grilled cheese recipe guaranteed to make you feel like a cook. Anybody can make this recipe and it takes only minutes to finish. The best part: you can use any type of cheese, bread, or butter.

Ingredients:

  • White bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Heat your pan
  2. While heating the pan, butter your bread
  3. Set one buttered bread slice on the pan
  4. Add cheese on top of slice in the pan
  5. Cover cheese with second bread slice
  6. Cook both sides until golden brown
  7. Optional: cut your grilled cheese in half
