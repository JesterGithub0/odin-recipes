Lasagna

Description:

A simple lasagna recipe that requires time and effort but minimal knowledge of cooking. Satisfy your cravings for lasagna with the best lasagna recipe you'll find online.

Ingredients:

  • Meats: italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products: paste, crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices: parsley, basil, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta
  • One egg

Steps:

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make cheese mixt ure
  4. Layer lasagna according to recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest
